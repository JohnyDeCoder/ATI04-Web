STEPS TO FOLLOW THE CULT

If you want to be part of the Cult of the Lamb, son of Narinder you must follow the 6 sacred commandments that Narinder left his son.

Remember, no temptation has overtaken you except what is common to mankind. And he is faithful; he will not let you be tempted beyond what you can bear!

# Commandment Description
1 Faith You must believe in the power of the Lamb and his ability to lead his followers to salvation.
2 Devotion You must make time each day to worship the Lamb, whether through prayer, meditation or service to others.
3 Community You must work in harmony with the other members of the cult, supporting them in their needs and sharing your resources with them.
4 Sacrifice You must be willing to make sacrifices for the good of worship, either by renouncing your material possessions or dedicating your time and energy to the service of the Lamb
5 Purity You must strive to maintain the purity of your mind and heart, avoiding negative thoughts and actions that can lead you away from the path of the Lamb.
6 Perseverance You must remain faithful to the Lamb even in times of difficulty, trusting that he will guide you through trials and tribulations.
